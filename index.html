Our Capabilities

Design

Our Graphic Design Team can help you develop a package that stands out in a crowd, showcase your brand image, product value, and sells.

Printing

Eye catching graphics with crisp and sharp colors that come alive. Our versatile and high-speed equipment provides seven color output and a wide variety of coating options including UV. Our machines allow for real-time color monitoring and content flaw detection.

Folding and Gluing

Folding and gluing transforms the box from a flat layout to of a folding carton and by doing so, can save buyers the time and cost of hand assembly costs.

Windowing

Display your product in a transparent window so it can be protected while offering consumers an opportunity to view the product.

Foiling

Foiling can add a high level of sophistication to a box and make it stand out in a crowd as product as something special.

Embossing and Debossing

Raise the graphics on your box to make it stand up and out in the market.

About Us

CMT Packaging & Designs, Inc. is an independent women owned custom folding carton company based in Edison, New Jersey. We Specialize in Food Packaging, Pharmaceuticals, Cosmetics and Toiletries, and packaging for other consumer products.

For 18-years, CMT Packaging & Designs has provided innovative packaging solutions to manufacturers of foods, pharmaceuticals, and a wide range of consumer products. From basic folding cartons to advanced packaging designs, CMT Packaging has built its reputation on quality products, innovative designs, excellent service, and competitive pricing.

More than a packaging company, CMT Packaging & Designs has the capability and experience to work with its customers in all phases of product development and commercialization.

From Protecting your Products to Promoting your Brands, CMT Packaging & Designs Provides Packaging Solutions that Sell.
